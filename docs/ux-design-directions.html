Design Direction Variations

Exploring the "Single Tone Active" Theme

Direction A

The Bento Grid

Structure, density, and immediate visual variety.

Hello, I'm Charlie.
Why: Instantly shows "breadth" of skills. Best for scanning. Very trendy in engineering portfolios (Linear style).
Direction B

The Narrative

Scrollytelling, typography-led, dramatic.

Building
Complexity
Simpler.

Why: Maximizes the "Hook". Feels like a product launch. Sells the *person* before the skills.
Direction C

The Terminal

Minimalist, monospaced, code-centric.

> const engineer = {
  name: "Charlie",
  passion: "Audio"
};
Why: Purest signal of "I write code." Very authoritative but potentially less "friendly" to non-technical recruiters.
